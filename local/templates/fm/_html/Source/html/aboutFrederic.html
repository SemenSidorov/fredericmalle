The Editions de Parfums Frédéric Malle were born in 2000 from Frédéric's will Trunk to give back to the Perfumery its letters of nobility.

He works with the greatest perfumers as with art craftsmen, their gives complete freedom to create, all means, raw materials exceptional, no limit of cost, time, or marketing constraints. Strong of more than thirty years of experience, Frédéric Malle brings his unique expertise, allows these artists to surpass themselves and encourages them to develop their own writing.

It results from this unique company a collection of fragrances that brings together all styles, all olfactory families, revisits the classics with modernity, and explores new territories. Exceptional creations, whose authors are so proud that they sign them by name for the first time.

The diversity of this collection allows everyone to find the scent that match, and assert his own style. Expression of your personality, weapon of seduction, your perfume accompanies you every day, in some opportunities and sometimes even for life.

Perfumes born from total creative freedom

Editions de Parfums Frédéric Malle is a publishing house for the industry’s very best Perfume Designers. Its goal is to push the limits of perfume creation and elevate perfumery to a whole new artistic level.
