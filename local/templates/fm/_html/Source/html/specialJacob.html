Perfume Designers

Frédéric Malle and his Perfume Designers want the same things: creative freedom, access to the highest quality raw materials, and time. Together they collaborate as authors would with an editor, constantly revisiting compositions to the point of perfection, and always striving to create the modern classics of tomorrow.

JAKOB + MACFARLANE

13, rue des Francs Bourgeois

I have always loved Jakob + MacFarlane for its work in the restaurant Georges at the Centre Pompidou in Paris.The restaurant remains, to this day, a futuristic fantasy, perfectly unexpected. It was not until I revisited the Florence Loewy bookstore in the Marais, home to Jakob + MacFarlane’s “hive” of plywood bookshelves, that I found inspiration for my own Marais boutique.

The architects and I subsequently joined forces and envisioned a place where imagination knows no bounds: a design that defies the laws of nature with a structure of floating islands that appear suspended in time. More precisely, a mirrored box, slipped into the existing shell of a historic building, where a cluster of buoyant islands could be reflected into infinity. The endless visual complexity produced by the mirrors is an invitation to reflect on your own, while choosing the perfume that defines you.
