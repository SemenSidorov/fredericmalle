The Publisher

Bruno Jovanovic is a raising star at IFF (International Flavor and Fragrances). Unlike many of his contemporaries, who create at the mercy of fragrance distribution, Bruno Jovanovic has developed a technique highly influenced by perfume classics. Similarly, his ideas for perfumes, which often appear as pictures or impressions of daily life in his mind, are ones of classic elegance; but his method, which seeks to modernize these images, is distinctly innovative.
